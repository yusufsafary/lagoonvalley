What has changed

Every update to Lagoon Valley, from the initial release onward. New builds go out as features are ready.

v0.2.0 June 2025 Latest Update

A round of quality-of-life improvements and new content built on player feedback from the first release. The tide system got a visual refresh and the market was expanded.

Added
  • Tide indicator now shows a countdown to the next tide shift
  • Two new crop varieties: blueberry and pumpkin
  • NPC Mira now has season-aware dialogue that changes based on the in-game day count
  • Soft particle effects on crop harvest for visual feedback
  • About, How to Play, and Changelog pages on the site
  • Favicon and OG image for social sharing
Changed
  • Joystick zone enlarged for easier thumb placement on larger phones
  • Coastal plots now show a subtle blue tint when high tide is approaching
  • Shop panel now scrolls smoothly on small screens
  • Action button label updates dynamically to show the current action (Till, Plant, Water, Harvest, Talk)
Fixed
  • Watered crops occasionally resetting to stage 1 after page reload
  • Action button registering double taps on fast devices
  • Shop close button misaligned on very narrow screens
v0.1.0 May 2025 First Release

The first public release of Lagoon Valley. A mobile-first 3D farming game built in Three.js with a working tide system, a crop economy, and an NPC who actually has something to say.

Shipped
  • Fully explorable 3D valley with coastal terrain and a working lagoon
  • Four-stage crop growth system: till, plant, water, harvest
  • Tide cycle that floods and unflooods coastal farm plots on a real timer
  • Real-time day and time of day cycle with HUD display
  • The Lagoon Market with buy and sell tabs
  • Four initial crop varieties: carrot, tomato, corn, watermelon
  • NPC Mira with contextual dialogue
  • Virtual joystick and action button controls for mobile
  • Inventory bar with slot selection
  • Loading screen with progress bar
  • Toast notification system for in-game feedback

What is coming next

These are features being worked on or planned for future releases. Nothing is confirmed until it ships, but this gives a sense of where the valley is heading.

Coming Soon

Fishing

Cast from the shore during low tide. Different fish at different times of day. Sell at the market like crops.

Coming Soon

More NPCs

Additional residents with their own knowledge of the valley. Each one tied to a different area of the land.

Planned

Seasons

A seasonal cycle that affects which crops grow and how often the tide shifts. Spring is calm. Storm season is not.

Planned

Save System

Persist your farm progress across sessions using local storage. No accounts needed.

Planned

Craftable Items

Combine harvested crops to create new items worth more at the market. A basic crafting loop.

Planned

Better Models

Replace the current primitive geometry with proper GLTF models for characters, crops, and buildings.
